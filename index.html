The Bride and Groom

Paths Collide

We first met way back in the 7th grade. While we had a few classes together and hung out with eachother and some of the same people, we were still very young and naive trying our best to navigate the awkwardness of middle school.

Paths Stray

Before we came together we drifted apart. Erica attended Sherwood Highschool and Stephen, Dematha Catholic, though we bumped into one another through mutual friends. College saw us farther still. Erica found herself deep in the Appalachian Mountains, earning a B.S. in Sociology from Radford University. Meanwhile, Stephen stayed close to home at UMBC earning himself a B.S. in Biochemistry & Molecular Biology. After our college adventures, Erica came back home and Stephen went away. He went down to Atlanta to earn his M.S. in Bioinformatics and she was working on Captiol Hill, rubbing shoulders with the political elite.

Paths Entwined

Unknown to us, we were both working in the DMV and even lived just a couple miles away from our future spouse. One day after work, we we decompressing in our respective apartments idly swiping through Tinder (don't say it's good for nothing!) when, "Hey...I know them!" we saw each other on the app. A few messages later and we set up a date (even though Erica wasn't sure it was at first)! Over the next few days and weeks it became obvious, this person is special. And the rest, is history :-)

Wedding #1

We moved in together into an apartment in Reston so Stephen could be closer to his job. Hardly a month after we moved in, COVID hit and no one could go into the office anymore. Being stuck together helped us grow closer even faster than we already were. We tied the knot during COVID in the backyard of a very kind notary and had a small celebration dinner with a couple close family members.
